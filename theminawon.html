On Christmas Day, I decided to take my family to “Ice!” at National Harbour-David came too. Afer, David said he needed to help his brother with something so he had to stop by home. My family and I were hungry so my parents suggested jja-jang-myun. I got really excited and starting chanting “jja-jang-myung! jja-jang-myun!” But then, David insisted that we go see the Christmas lights around our neighborhood the way we planned. I said I was hungry and that the Christmas lights will still be there even when we come back from dinner. He kept being persistent about me not going to eat, which left me frustrated and confused about how eating dinner could possibly mess up our previous plans. After all, the Christmas lights weren’t going anywhere.

We decided to eat at home instead, and then David picked me up. We drove We drove around the neighborhood and other neighborhoods to look at pretty lights just like we did the year before. The only thing missing really- was hot chocolate like we previously planned. He told me that there were some really pretty ones close to his house that his roommates suggested, so we drove towards his place.

As we got closer to his house, he started talking about ‘How I Met Your Mother,’ one of the TV Shows we watched religiously. We still haven’t gotten a chance to see the most recent episode together. He said that he had it downloaded and also found this secret episode that had clips of future episode of HIMYM. We decided to stop by his place and watch the show before heading out again to look at more houses.

We drove up to his driveway, and what do you know? There was Minzy, my little sister, and Josh, David’s little brother, waiting for us with hot chocolate! After we cheered, they took me to his backyard, which had a beautifully lit pathway and a bonfire! We sat down and that’s when he showed me ‘The Secret Episode.’

I said “yes.”

Then he surprised me with the presence of all my amazing friends who took their time to celebrate with us on Christmas Day!

It was the most perfect proposal. It was so Mina-like and everything had such a sentimental touch. I honestly could not have thought of a better proposal for myself. Words cannot express how touched I was--I was especially humbled by how much love and support we received from our family and friends. David and I are so blessed to have such a wonderful community. Thank you all for your encouraging words and prayers!
